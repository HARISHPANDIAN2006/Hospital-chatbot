# Hospital Laboratory Test Directory

This document contains a comprehensive list of laboratory and diagnostic tests available at the hospital, including preparation requirements, costs, and clinical relevance.


## Hematology Tests

### Complete Blood Count (CBC)
- **Category:** Hematology
- **Normal Range:** Hemoglobin: Male 13.5-17.5 | Female 12-16; WBC: 4000-11000; Platelet: 150000-450000""
- **Unit:** g/dL
- **Fasting Required:**  cells/μL""
- **Sample Type:** No
- **Report Time:** Blood
- **Cost:** ₹Same day
- **Clinical Significance:** 400
- **Notes:** Detects anemia

### ESR (Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate)
- **Category:** Hematology
- **Normal Range:** Male: 0-15; Female: 0-20""
- **Unit:** mm/hr
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹150
- **Clinical Significance:** Inflammation marker""
- **Notes:** Non-specific test for inflammation""

### Prothrombin Time (PT/INR)
- **Category:** Hematology
- **Normal Range:** PT: 11-13.5 sec; INR: 0.8-1.1""
- **Unit:** Seconds
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹300
- **Clinical Significance:** Blood clotting assessment""
- **Notes:** Important for patients on blood thinners""

### D-Dimer
- **Category:** Hematology
- **Normal Range:** <500
- **Unit:** ng/mL
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** 4-6 hours
- **Cost:** ₹1500
- **Clinical Significance:** Blood clot detection""
- **Notes:** High in DVT and pulmonary embolism""


## Biochemistry Tests

### Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS)
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** 70-100
- **Unit:** mg/dL
- **Fasting Required:** Yes (8-12 hrs)
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹150
- **Clinical Significance:** Diabetes screening and monitoring""
- **Notes:** Fast overnight; no food or drinks except water""

### HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin)
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** Normal: <5.7%; Prediabetes: 5.7-6.4%; Diabetes: ≥6.5%""
- **Unit:** Percentage
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹600
- **Clinical Significance:** 3-month average blood sugar control""
- **Notes:** Gold standard for diabetes management""

### Lipid Profile
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** Total Cholesterol: <200; LDL: <100; HDL: >40 (M) >50 (F); Triglycerides: <150""
- **Unit:** mg/dL
- **Fasting Required:** Yes (10-12 hrs)
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹500
- **Clinical Significance:** Cardiovascular risk assessment""
- **Notes:** Avoid alcohol 24 hrs before test""

### Liver Function Test (LFT)
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** SGOT: 10-40; SGPT: 10-40; Bilirubin: 0.3-1.2; Total Protein: 6-8.3""
- **Unit:** U/L and g/dL
- **Fasting Required:** Yes (6-8 hrs)
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹550
- **Clinical Significance:** Liver health assessment""
- **Notes:** Avoid alcohol 48 hrs before""

### Kidney Function Test (KFT)
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** Creatinine: 0.6-1.2; BUN: 7-20; Uric Acid: 3.5-7.2""
- **Unit:** mg/dL
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹500
- **Clinical Significance:** Kidney function evaluation""
- **Notes:** Stay well hydrated""

### Electrolytes (Na K Cl)
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** Sodium: 135-145; Potassium: 3.5-5.0; Chloride:96-106""
- **Unit:** mEq/L
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** Same day
- **Cost:** ₹350
- **Clinical Significance:** Fluid and electrolyte balance""
- **Notes:** Critical in dehydration cases""

### Vitamin B12
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** Normal: 200-900; Deficient: <200""
- **Unit:** pg/mL
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** 48 hours
- **Cost:** ₹800
- **Clinical Significance:** Nerve health and blood formation""
- **Notes:** Deficiency common in vegetarians""

### C-Reactive Protein (CRP)
- **Category:** Biochemistry
- **Normal Range:** <1 (Low risk); 1-3 (Moderate); >3 (High)
- **Unit:** mg/L
- **Fasting Required:** No
- **Sample Type:** Blood
- **Report Time:** 24 hours
- **Cost:** ₹400
- **Clinical Significance:** Inflammation and cardiac risk marker""
- **Notes:** More specific than ESR""
