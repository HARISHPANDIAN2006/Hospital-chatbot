# Apollo Medicity Hospital - Overview

## About Us

Apollo Medicity Hospital is a 450-bed multi-specialty tertiary care hospital located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1998, we are committed to providing world-class healthcare services with compassion and excellence.

## Our Mission

To bring healthcare of international standards within the reach of every individual through clinical excellence, advanced technology, and compassionate care.

## Emergency Services

Our Emergency Department operates 24/7 with:
- Dedicated emergency physicians
- Trauma care specialists
- Advanced life support ambulances
- Direct access to all specialties
- Fast-track diagnostic services

**When to Visit Emergency:**
- Chest pain or difficulty breathing
- Severe injuries or accidents
- Unconsciousness or seizures
- Severe bleeding
- Suspected stroke symptoms
- Severe allergic reactions
- Poisoning or overdose
